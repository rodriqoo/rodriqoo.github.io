HACK A HUMAN

We live in an era where the digital has taken over our lives and our world. It is important for everyone to be aware of the direction that artificial intelligence is taking.


If we don’t control it we might end up living in a dystopian society where machines have surpassed the human kind.


On top of artificial intelligence, this project revolves around the future of algorithms and data management.


Algorithms and data nowadays tell us what movies to watch or what products to buy, but in a few decades, it can go as far as deciding what to study, where to work, whom to marry or even whom to vote for.

ESCAPISM

SIR DUKE

The designer was invited to participate in an exhibition of musical commemorative films that are being recognized for having marked the last decades of the past century.


In this context, it was proposed to you to create an animated film that reflects the music attributed in emotions, rhythm, story and lyrics.


For this, the designer did a research about the decade in which the music is inserted, its history and relevance, created a moodboard, a storyboard, an animatic and the final animation.

AUGMENTED UNREALITY

Contemporary art and design have undergone major changes with the introduction of techonlogy in their practices. The way technology relates to design is diverse and, although most have adopted it as an integral part of their practice, some choose to consciously resist its use.


The main topic for this project is “The role of Technology in Art and Design”, and the selected sub-topic is “Augmented Reality”.


With the increasing growth of this technology through the years, many companies began to use this method in their campaigns and products, which brought a larger interaction between the customer and the design itself.


This was a collaboration project with Eva Falcão and Rodrigo Estevão where we used augmented reality as an approach method to our main theme of 2020 RECAP. We created and animated posters with 15 of the biggest events of the year.

CINEMATECA

The designer was invited to redesign the graphic identity of the Cinemateca Portuguesa - Museu do Cinema.


Thus, it was proposed to develop a new brand strategy with the respective digital standards manual and all the graphic communication with the respective style guide.


To do this, the designer started by researching everything about the institution and its brand, its history, values and relevance and make a conclusive analysis that allows you to advance
to the next stages.


TWO SIDES

An Independent Publisher challenged the Designer to develop an Editorial project around the theme/ /concept “Dichotomy”.


The project had an analysis phase, research on the topic and on other similar or relevant projects, elaboration of mental, visual and verbal maps.


The Designer developed his concept / proposal and gather all the content to develop his / her Editorial project and produced a prototype of his proposal as close as possible to the proposed final result.


HELVETICA IDEAL

IDEAL cinema organized a replacement of the film Helvetica as part of a cinema cycle
dedicated to Design.


For the presentation of the film, IDEAL cinema invited the writer and designer Toby Shorin for a conversation about the film.


The cinema intended to publicize the film’s replacement through a series of seven posters/folders that promote the film and the conversation simultaneously.


Cinema sought to ensure that posters had an experimental and procedural approach capable of convoking different languages and visual references.
